Base Form

Goku's standard form. No additional power-ups or transformations applied.

Kaioken

A technique that multiplies Goku's power for a short period of time, at the cost of physical strain.

Super Saiyan

Goku's first major transformation, granting him a significant power boost with golden hair and green eyes.

Super Saiyan 2

An advanced form of Super Saiyan with increased power and a more intense aura.

Super Saiyan 3

An even more powerful transformation with long hair and increased energy, but it consumes a lot of stamina.

Super Saiyan God

A transformation achieved through divine energy, giving Goku red hair and a calm demeanor.

Super Saiyan Blue

Also known as Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan, this form combines Super Saiyan with God-like power and blue hair.

Ultra Instinct

A state where Goku's body moves instinctively, allowing him to react and fight with incredible speed and precision.
